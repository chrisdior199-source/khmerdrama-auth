Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to permanently delete your TosMer account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

1
Send an email to our support team Email us at support.tosmer@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
2
Include your account information Please include the phone number or Telegram username linked to your TosMer account so we can identify and delete the correct account.
3
Wait for confirmation We will process your request and send a confirmation email within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Action
Phone number / Telegram ID Permanently deleted
Display name and avatar Permanently deleted
Watch history Permanently deleted
Favorites and likes Permanently deleted
Points balance and transaction history Permanently deleted
Comments and reviews Permanently deleted
VIP subscription status Permanently deleted
Firebase authentication record Permanently deleted
Share history Permanently deleted
Danmaku (floating comments) Anonymized (username removed)
⚠️ Please Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any unused points, unlocked episodes, or active VIP subscription will be forfeited and cannot be refunded. If you have an active paid VIP subscription, please contact us before requesting deletion to discuss your options.

Retention Period

After your deletion request is confirmed, all personal data will be removed within 7 business days. Anonymized, non-identifiable data (such as aggregate view counts) may be retained for service improvement purposes.
